Seek the Signs

As many may not know, many children who experience abuse will not display physical signs. Child abuse can be physical, sexual, emotional, or neglect, many of which do not leave clear markings. Even so, you should still look out for signs. These signs include :

  • Behavioral changes
  • Increase in anxiety and fear
  • Decrease in school preformance
  • Regression in development
  • Trouble sleeping
  • Trouble getting along with siblings, family, friends, etc.
  • Loss of interest in usual activities

If you suspsect a child to be being abused based on these signs, please contact one of the resources on our Seek Help Page
